Our Inception

Officially established by the Universiti Malaya Senate and Board of Directors on March 6, 2026, the Fakulti Kejururawatan (Faculty of Nursing) represents a major milestone in strengthening nursing academic development in Malaysia.

Created as a strategic and dedicated initiative to address the critical shortage of nurses, the faculty aims to elevate the nursing profession through rigorous academic and clinical training.

"Menyambut baik inisiatif penubuhan Fakulti Kejururawatan sebagai sebahagian daripada usaha Universiti memperkukuh pembangunan akademik dalam bidang kejururawatan."
